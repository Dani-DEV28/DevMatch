# DevMatch — 60-90 Second Video Demo Script

**Format:** Screen recording with voiceover (no face cam needed)
**Tool:** QuickTime screen record, Loom, or OBS
**Resolution:** 1920x1080, browser at 100% zoom
**Post to:** X (Twitter) and LinkedIn

---

## SETUP BEFORE RECORDING

- [ ] App running on localhost:3000, logged out
- [ ] Demo data seeded
- [ ] Browser: hide bookmarks bar, close all other tabs
- [ ] One clean browser window, no extensions visible
- [ ] Audio: quiet room, speak close to mic
- [ ] Have a Luma or Meetup event URL ready to paste (or just type manually)

---

## THE SCRIPT

### COLD OPEN — Screen on login page (0:00 - 0:08)

*Show the DevMatch login page*

> "You walk into a meetup with 50 developers and have no idea who to talk to. DevMatch fixes that."

**Click "Login with GitHub."**

---

### LOGIN + DASHBOARD (0:08 - 0:25)

*OAuth redirects back to dashboard. Pause to let it load.*

> "Sign in with GitHub. Your profile auto-populates — name, avatar, skills, all pulled from your repos."

*Mouse over your profile card on the left side.*

> "DevMatch analyzes your GitHub activity and assigns you a builder archetype — your Digital DNA."

*Move mouse to the match feed on the right.*

> "Then it finds developers who align with your values and vision — not just your tech stack."

*Hover over the top match card, showing the score and badges.*

> "Every match is scored by vibe alignment, vision resonance, and behavioral signals. And it tells you exactly why you matched."

---

### CLICK INTO A PROFILE (0:25 - 0:35)

*Click the top match card to open their profile page.*

> "Click into a match and you see the full picture — their personality, shared skills, Digital DNA, and project visions."

*Slowly scroll down the profile to show the personality card, skills with stars, Digital DNA bars.*

**Click "Back to Matches" to return to dashboard.**

---

### EVENT SESSIONS (0:35 - 0:55)

*Click "Events" in the nav bar.*

> "Here's where it gets real. Create an event session for tonight's meetup."

*Click "Create Event." Type a name like "Code & Coffee March Meetup" or paste a Luma URL.*

> "Paste a Luma or Meetup link and the event details auto-fill. Every event gets a unique join code."

*Submit the form. Click into the created event.*

> "Share the code at the meetup. Attendees join and now you see your top matches from only the people in the room."

*Mouse over the scoped matches area and participant list.*

> "Not the whole platform. Just the people here, tonight, who share your vision."

---

### CLOSE (0:55 - 1:05)

*Switch back to the dashboard (click DevMatch logo or Dashboard link).*

> "DevMatch. Vision-first developer matching. Built in 5 hours by 3 devs with Qoder and InsForge."

*Let the dashboard sit on screen for 2-3 seconds, then end recording.*

## RECORDING TIPS

- **Pace:** Move deliberately. Don't rush clicks. Let pages load fully before speaking about them.
- **Mouse:** Move smoothly, don't circle or jiggle. Point at what you're talking about.
- **Voice:** Conversational, not scripted. It's fine to paraphrase — the script is a guide, not a teleprompter.
- **Edits:** If you mess up, just pause, then re-say the line. Cut the mistake in post. A single clean take is ideal but not required.
- **Length:** Aim for 70 seconds. Under 60 feels rushed. Over 90 loses attention.
- **Silence:** 1-2 seconds of silence while something loads is fine. Don't fill it with "um."
